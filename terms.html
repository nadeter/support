Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

We believe that your personal data belongs to you. Our app is built with a privacy-first architecture. This policy explains how we handle information in our "Freemium" model.

2. Data Collection and Storage

The core principle of our app is that we do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

  • Local Storage: All data you create is stored exclusively on your device.
  • No Accounts: You do not need to create an account to use the app.
  • No Tracking: We do not use third-party trackers or analytics that identify you.

3. Data Control and Deletion

Since all data remains on your device, you have full control:

  • You can modify or delete your data at any time within the app.
  • Deleting the app from your device will automatically erase all data associated with it.

4. Apple’s Services (Freemium Model)

To provide "Freemium" features, we use Apple’s standard services:

  • In-App Purchases: All transactions are processed by Apple via the App Store. We do not have access to your credit card information or billing details.
  • iCloud (Optional): If you enable iCloud sync, your data is stored in your private Apple iCloud account. We cannot access this data.

5. Third-Party Links

The app may contain links to the App Store for ratings or to our support page. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external sites.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a new "Effective Date."
